Welcome to my website! I am an Assistant Professor (maître de conférences) at the University of Paris-Panthéon-Assas, a member of LEMMA, and an associate researcher at GATE Lyon Saint-Étienne.

I work on public economics and political economy, combining theory, econometrics, and experiments to study taxation, redistribution, local governance, and voting.
